Get to know me better

First of all, let me say that my name was given to me by my father in honor of the famous Armenian king, Tigran the Great

I was born on October 28, 1990, in the city of Vedi, Republic of Armenia.

Although I grew up during difficult times, my childhood was happy, playful, and filled with memorable experiences. I was an energetic, active, and mischievous child.

Since everything came easily to me, I developed various hobbies such as singing, music, dancing, painting, sports, science, and art.

I had a particular passion for solving logical problems, a love that remains with me to this day.

After graduating from school in 2007, I enrolled at the State Engineering University of Armenia.

In 2022, I made the decision to seriously pursue programming and immediately fell in love with this profession. It provides a wide range of opportunities for creation, which I thoroughly enjoy regardless of my location.

That's a brief overview of me.
